# Activate/Deactivate Agreements

Activate or deactivate your agreement transactions.

Use this feature to activate or deactivate a group of agreement transactions. Access the feature as follows:

1.  Log in to your application.
2.  Go to *Design* \> *B2B Scenarios*.
3.  Choose the *Cross Actions* tab and select *Activate/Deactivate Agreements*.
4.  The *Choose Action* dialog box opens allowing you to choose between the following:
    -   [Activate Agreements](activate-deactivate-agreements-e068e37.md#loioe068e375c6014bb4ba7c0d1e0e0733ad__section_dct_jmp_zxb)
    -   [Deactivate Agreements](activate-deactivate-agreements-e068e37.md#loioe068e375c6014bb4ba7c0d1e0e0733ad__section_jyy_hpp_zxb)

## Activate Agreements

Follow the following procedure to activate agreements:

1.  Enter a meaningful name in the *Action Name* field.
2.  Provide a description in the *Description* field and choose *Create*.
3.  Select the agreement that you want to activate from the drop-down list of the *Agreement Name* field and select *Go*.

    > ### Note:  
    > You can choose more than one agreement.
    > 
    > You can filter your search further by adding the trading partner, user email address, and the last modified date in their respective fields.
    > 
    > By default, the *Activation Status* is set to *Inactive* to display only the agreements that are in an inactive state.

4.  The filter results are displayed in the *Agreements* table. Select the list of agreements for activation.

    > ### Restriction:  
    > Activate a **maximum of 300 agreements** at once. Activating more than 300 agreements can cause performance issues.

    > ### Note:  
    > The agreement list displays only `inactive` agreement groups.
    > 
    > If at least one agreement group has more than one version, the agreement list is displayed with grouping. Otherwise, the list is displayed without grouping.
    > 
    > Only one version can be selected for activation, even if an agreement group has more than one version, and only selected agreement groups are activated.

5.  Choose *Activate*.
6.  The *Actions Logs* table displays the progress of your task. Choose and open your log to view the task in detail.

## Deactivate Agreements

Follow the following procedure to deactivate agreements:

1.  Enter a meaningful name in the *Action Name* field.
2.  Provide a description in the *Description* field and choose *Create*.
3.  Select the agreement that you want to deactivate from the drop-down list of the *Agreement Name* field and select *Go*.

    > ### Note:  
    > You can choose more than one agreement.
    > 
    > You can filter your search further by adding the trading partner, user email address, and the last modified date in their respective fields.
    > 
    > By default, the *Activation Status* is set to *Active* to display only the agreements that are in active state.

4.  The filter results are displayed in the *Agreements* table. Select the list of agreements for deactivation.

    > ### Note:  
    > The agreement list displays only `Active` agreement groups.
    > 
    > The list is displayed without grouping since each active agreements group has only one active version.
    > 
    > Only selected agreement groups are activated.

5.  Choose *Deactivate*.
6.  The *Actions Logs* table displays the progress of your task. Choose and open your log to view the task in detail.
